contests

Yes! I love to run contests for my fans where you can win cool prizes…like a $50 gift certificate, autographed copies of my print books, and many other things. I also love to involve my readers in my writing process by running contests to pick the name of my next character, or the setting for the next book. Here are some of the contests and opportunities for my fans I have running right now.

  • To celebrate the release of the newest book from the Spirit Walker Series, Buried Alive, from now until the end of March 2022, if you purchase a copy of book 1, Killing Dreams, or book 2, Buried Alive I will add your name to the dedication section of book 3, The Stolen. Just send either a picture of your new purchase whether it be print or digital, or the receipt for your purchase to romance@donnettesmith.com When you do, I’ll let you know I received it. It’s as easy as that.
  • And since I can’t decide if we should continue to book 4 of the Spirit Walker Series, or start a new adventure with different characters, I am leaving it up to my fans to decide if it’s time for another series, or if you’re just not finished with Jenna and Cole yet. From the beginning of March 2022, until April 15th, if you’d like to express your opinion on this important decision, please email me at romance@donnettesmith.com and let me know what you think. The ball is in your court. Choose wisely.
  • I’ve got lots of awesome giveaways that will be taking place as part of my upcoming book tour. You can check it out here (Jake, put a link to the book tour button on the word here)
